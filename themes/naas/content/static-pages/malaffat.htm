As the digital publishing arm of NAAS, Malaffat is dedicated to documenting and preserving narratives and lived experiences around Arab cinema culture. It features a series of curated collections of contributions (Dossiers) that explore the histories, challenges, and practices shaping cinema in our region and regions of affinity.
